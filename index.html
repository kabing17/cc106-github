About PlastiClear

PlastiClear is a mobile-based system that helps households, waste collectors, and junkshops identify, sort, and price recyclable plastics efficiently.

Key Features

  • Plastic Identification
  • Real-Time Junkshop Pricing
  • Proper Waste Sorting Guide
  • Nearby Junkshop Locator
